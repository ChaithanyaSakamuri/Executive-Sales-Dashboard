## ‚ú® Key Features & Visualizations

The dashboard is structured around several key performance indicators (KPIs) and detailed visualizations:

| KPI | Value (2015) | Insight |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Total Sales** | $712K | The overall revenue generated in the year. |
| **Total Profit** | $75K | The net profit across all transactions. |
| **Profit Ratio** | 11% | A strong indicator of overall sales efficiency. |
| **Total Orders** | 438 | The volume of transactions processed. |

### Visual Components:

* **Sales by State/Region:** A geographical map showing sales distribution across the United States.
* **Sales by Segment (Donut Chart):** Highlights the percentage contribution of customer segments (e.g., Corporate, Home Office, Consumer) to the total sales volume.
    * *Key Finding:* **[Corporate]** is the largest segment by sales.
* **Sales & Profit by Sub-Category (Bar Chart):** Compares total sales and profit side-by-side for product sub-categories.
    * *Key Finding:* **Office Machines** and **Telephones and Communication** are major profit centers.
* **Profit Trend (Line Chart):** Illustrates the monthly profit fluctuations throughout 2015 to identify seasonality or key operational events.
* **Sales by Ship Mode & Profit by Category (Bar Charts):** Provides quick insights into logistics efficiency and the most profitable product groups.
    * *Key Finding:* The **Technology** product category yields the highest profit.
* **Discount vs Sales Analysis (Waterfall/Bar Chart):** Shows the relationship between discount levels and sales value, revealing optimal or problematic discount strategies.
